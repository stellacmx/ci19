What is Formalism?




Formalism is the study of art by analyzing and comparing form and style. Its discussion also includes their purely visual or material aspects.









How I interpret Formalism?




Formalism makes graphic designers explore the usages of pure graphic elements on a 2D surface instead of simply delivering practical information, which I think is the most important job to focus on as a graphic designer, and it directly reflects the ability of the designer.
